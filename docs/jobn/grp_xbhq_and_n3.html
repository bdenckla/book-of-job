Quirks not transcribed by BHQ

The Job fascicle of BHQ does not transcribe 33 quirks in μL that are noted in one or more of the other three editions.

Each entry below takes the following form:

proposed
consensus		 # c:v how proposed differs from consensus

Where:

  • proposed is a proposed transcription of μL or, rarely, μA
  • consensus is the Masoretic consensus reading (or a good guess of it)
  • # (hash sign) (number sign) is a link to more details on this quirk
  • c:v is the chapter and verse of the book of Job

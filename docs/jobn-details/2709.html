הֲֽ֭צַעֲקָתוֹ		 27:9 פתח on ה is not חטף in μL,
says BHQ’s implication

As DM footnote 20 mentions, this is one of three such cases, the other two being 9:35 and 34:33. In all three cases, the consensus has געיה on an initial vocal שווא notated as a חטף פתח. In μL, the געיה is on an initial פתח, a full (albeit short) syllable rather than a שווא. In μA it is difficult to tell whether the פתח is חטף, but my guess is that it is not, i.e. my guess is that it matches μL in this respect. — U — M — μL (page 404B, col 1, line 7):

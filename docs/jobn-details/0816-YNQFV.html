Above I have asserted that the consensus has געיה here, but this געיה, like most cases of געיה, is optional. Therefore, one could just as easily assert the consensus has no געיה. I have chosen the consensus to be as close as possible to the proposed transcription of μL. I did this in order to make the proposed transcription seem no weirder than it really is. In other words, it is better to frame this as a question of דחי versus געיה than as a question of דחי versus no mark at all.
